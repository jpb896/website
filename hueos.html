hueOS

The lightweight modern OS for your everyday.

hueOS is an open source OS based on the Android mobile platform where modernity and speed are at the forefront.

Also in front and center is security: security updates are provided to every standard support version for at least a year after the release of a new version, while LTS releases can get up to 8 years of security support.
